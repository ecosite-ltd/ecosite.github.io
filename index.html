Our Mission

Transforming the way commercial solar is deployed across the UK and Ireland

The Problem

Commercial solar deployment is held back by inefficient prospecting. Sales teams waste countless hours on manual site identification, often pursuing unsuitable leads while missing high-potential opportunities.

Why It Matters

Every day of delay means lost carbon savings, higher customer acquisition costs, and slower clean energy deployment. The UK needs to accelerate commercial solar adoption to meet net-zero targets.

Our Solution

NowPV uses intelligent technology to identify and qualify commercial solar opportunities in seconds. From geospatial analysis to CRM integration, we streamline the entire prospecting workflow.
